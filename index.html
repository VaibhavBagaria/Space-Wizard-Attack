Space Wizard Attack




This is my first scratch game with hardwork. I learned scratch in 7th grade and I used it to create this first amazing game.



Source Code:Scratch|| Game credit:Vaibhav Bagaria

Instructions

Press Green Flag to Restart



Rules


Help the Wizard defeat the dragon


The health of both dragon and wizard are shown below


Press right key to shoot and space to defend


Make sure the dragon's fire ball doesn't hit your player(wizard) or else the dragon will get a extra 5 health


You have 1 minute to defeat the dragon


You have only 30 balls
